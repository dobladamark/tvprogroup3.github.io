Kalbaryo

Location

Libid, Binangonan Rizal

Kalbaryo will lead you to sanctify your soul by reflecting on your life while you slowly thread your way to the top.

About Rizal's Escapades

Rizal's Escapades, your gateway to the hidden treasures and thrilling adventures of Rizal province, Philippines. Discover the vibrant culture and breathtaking landscapes of Rizal through our curated selection of must-visit destinations.

Experience the culinary delights of Siblings Corner, a beloved eatery in Binangonan, offering delicious fast foods and refreshing drinks.
Indulge in the tranquil ambiance and stunning views at Sikatan Cafe, a renowned coffee shop overlooking Laguna de Bay.

For spiritual rejuvenation and awe-inspiring vistas, embark on a pilgrimage to Kalbaryo in Brgy. Libid, Binangonan, and ascend the 288 steps to the summit of Mt. Calvary.

Finally, satisfy your cravings at Alona's Silog House, located along National Road in Barangay Palangoy, Binangonan, renowned for its extensive menu and affordable prices. At Rizal's Escapades, we're committed to showcasing the best of Rizal, inviting you to embark on unforgettable adventures and create lasting memories in this captivating province.
